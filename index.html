I am Yashwanth Gowda, an engineering professional working at the intersection of robotics, autonomous systems, machine learning, and cybersecurity/GRC. My background combines physical systems, control logic, software, data-driven intelligence, and security-aware system design.

On the robotics side, I enjoy building systems that move from simulation to reality: mapping environments, reading sensors, planning motion, controlling hardware, and validating behavior through testing. My technical work includes ROS2, SLAM, Nav2, control systems, sensor integration, simulation, and embedded robotics.

On the cybersecurity side, I bring hands-on GRC and security operations exposure from internship work involving ISO 27001-aligned governance, security audits, risk assessment, phishing monitoring, Microsoft Defender 365, KDMARC, SAFEScore.ai, and Infosec IQ workflows.

What connects both areas is systems thinking. I am interested in building and supporting connected technologies that are not only functional and intelligent, but also reliable, auditable, resilient, and secure.
